Bash Love Letter

Dearest Bash,

As soon as I saw you, I knew you'd be trouble. There you were, sitting with that eager look of yours. You didnt do much, but I immediately fell into a deep and uncomfortable trance.

The truth is, bash, I didnt like you at first. You look weird and can be confusing at times. But, I just cant get enough of you. You make it way too easy to create a new folder by simply entering mkdir. You always take me where I want to go when I enter cd [folder]. When you list everything within the folder with ls-l, I nearly lose my breath. Because of you, I'm never lost when I can just type pwd, showing exactly where I am.

One of your best attributes is that you do a lot of work for me. Like when I hit the tab key and you auto-complete files and various folder names, so I dont have to. I can always tell your listining by entering echo and you repeat exactly what I say.

Although you have a face that only a mother could love, I'd happily be your mother.

Love,

Brady
